Concert Gallery Anime Situation

The technology behind putting together a Vocaloid concert is actually very cool. Another wonderful thing about Vocaloid concerts/ Anime concerts is that it celebrates the wonderful music made by so many different producers. The company in charge of Vocaloid, Crypton, finds what Vocaloid songs are the most popular online and contacts the producers who made them to ask if they can include those songs in their concerts. Every song played at a Vocaloid concert is accompanied by real musicians as well as the holographic Vocaloid doing unique dances while singing. It might sound silly, but it can create a really awesome effect altogether!
