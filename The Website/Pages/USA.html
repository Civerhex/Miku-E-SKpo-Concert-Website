A concert is a performance of musics, examples a short concert of piano music, ect. A concert in British English is a performance of music by players or singers that does not involve theatrical staging compare recital. As modifier, a concert version of an opera. A concert in American English is to arrange or settle by mutual understanding; contrive or plan together; devise; mutual agreement; concord; harmony of action; musical consonance; a program of vocal or instrumental music, usually one in which a number of musicians perform together.
